Nuzleaf Guide

The Wily Pokemon

Nuzleaf is a toddler sized pokemon, characterized by its large leaf and mask like face.
The following should help inform new trainers on how to deal with this pokemon.

Typage

Nuzleaf is a Grass/Dark type pokemon. As a dual type Pokemon, it is more complex to figure out what it is weak and strong against
Nuzleaf have the following Strengths and Weaknesses

Offensive Description
Strong (x2) Ghost, Psychic, Water, Ground
Weak (x.5) Dark, Fighting, Fairy, Bug, Fire, , Poison, Dragon, Steel
Defensive Description
Weak (x2) Bug(4x!!), Fighting, , Ice, Fire, Fairy, Poison
Resists (x.5) Ground, Water, Grass, Electric, Ghost, Dark
Immune to (x0) Psychic

Nuzleaf is a semi-common pokemon known for playing its leaf in a whistle like manner to ward off other creatures. The pokemon has been known to fall on similar move sets, but varieties have been recorded. Examples are hard to come by, but Shiftry, what Nuzleaf evolves to, uses similar move sets. Examples are given Here, Here, and Here.
Feint Attack and Foul Play are preferred moves for Nuzleaf, but it is able to learn a variety of other moves. These moves are not always dark or grass type moves, so be sure to have a variety of types to help avoid any Knocked out Pokemon in battle. A common moveset is the following

  1. Feint Attack
  2. Grass Knot
  3. Leaf Blade
  4. Foul Play

However, these are not the only moves a Darkrai can learn. Some of the following have been seen in Nuzleaf

  • Frustration
  • Return
