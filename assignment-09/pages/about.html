How to Paint a Hat

Why do we use fabric paint?

Fabric paint doesn't flake off like acrylic does when heavily manipulated. This means your hat will last longer!

Can I paint things other than hats?

yYou can paint anything fabric with this in mind! Just keep in mind what's wet and what's dry

What if I really want to eat the paint?

Please don't eat the paint.
